We are the Afghanistan's Largest Team

The Faculty of Administration and Public Policy was established in 1390 in the framework of Kabul University. Since its establishment, the Department of Administration and Public Policy has been engaged in the training of expert staff in the fields of policymaking, governance, and management of government development programs. In the first year of its operation, the Faculty of Administration and Public Policy had 100 students and in the following years, it was increased with the increase of manpower. In 1400, it attracted 327 students through the competitive entrance exam process. It is worth mentioning that this university has so far been able to present eight rounds of graduates to the society at the bachelor's level.
